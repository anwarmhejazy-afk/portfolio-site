Professional Profile

I am a results-driven professional with a background spanning customer service, client relations, accounts support, financial operations, technical support, and education consultancy. Over the years, I have worked in roles that required communication, accuracy, service mindset, relationship management, and the ability to perform under pressure.

My experience across the UAE and the UK has strengthened my ability to adapt to different work environments, interact with diverse clients, and maintain a dependable, professional approach in both operational and customer-facing roles.

In addition to my business and service background, I have also worked on digital platforms and content projects, including consultancy websites, app concepts, and educational and finance-focused media channels. This gives me broader value than a traditional single-function profile.

I hold a Master of Science in International Business Management from the University of Sunderland, United Kingdom, complemented by a Bachelor of Business Administration in Banking and Finance from the University of Jazeera, Dubai. Together, these qualifications support a strong academic foundation in business, finance, management, and international professional practice.
